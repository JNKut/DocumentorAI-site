FAQ

Common questions

Still have questions? Email us at jonahkutikov@gmail.com.

How do I get started?
Email us at jonahkutikov@gmail.com and tell us which plan you're interested in. We'll get your chatbot configured and deployed within 24 hours — no technical knowledge needed on your end.
What documents can I use to train the AI?
You can upload PDF files, Word documents (.docx), or plain text files (.txt). Great candidates include your FAQ page, product catalog, policies, pricing guide, or any support documentation you already have.
How does the chatbot get embedded on my website?
We give you a single line of code to paste into your website. It works on Shopify, Webflow, WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, or any custom site. The chat widget appears in the bottom-right corner of your site automatically.
Can I update the knowledge base after setup?
Yes — you can upload a new document at any time through your admin panel. Updates go live instantly. There's no limit on how often you can do this.
What counts as a "conversation"?
A conversation is a single session where a visitor opens the chat widget and sends at least one message. Multiple back-and-forth messages within the same session count as one conversation.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime by emailing us. There are no cancellation fees.
What is white-label on the Agency plan?
White-label means the chatbot shows your company's name and branding — not "DocumentorAI." This is ideal for agencies that want to resell the product under their own brand to their clients.
